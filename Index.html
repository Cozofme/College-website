Doranda College

Doranda College offers rigorous academic programs, exceptional support systems, and top-notch faculty to ensure quality education. It aims to combat inequality by providing access to education for all, especially the weaker sections. The college has a dedicated arts and commerce building and a newly built science block funded by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of Jharkhand. Experienced faculty create a dynamic academic environment, blending tradition with innovation for a solid foundation in every field.

BSC-IT

BSc (Bachelor of Science) in IT is basically about storing, processing, securing, and managing information. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

BBA

The BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) course is a full-time three years (six semesters) undergraduate program. This degree provides foundational knowledge in business management and administration, preparing students for various managerial roles in the corporate world.

BA

The BA (Bachelor of Arts) course is a full-time three years (six semesters) undergraduate program. This degree offers a broad education in the humanities, social sciences, and liberal arts, allowing students to specialize in subjects like literature, history, psychology, and more.

BCom

The BCom (Bachelor of Commerce) course is a full-time three years (six semesters) undergraduate program. This degree focuses on commerce-related subjects such as accounting, finance, economics, and business law, preparing students for careers in commerce, finance, and accounting.
